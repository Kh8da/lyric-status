# Security

## Your Discord Token

Lyric Status stores your Discord user token locally in `.config.json`.

- The token **never leaves your machine** — it is only sent directly to `discord.com` for status updates
- `.config.json` is listed in `.gitignore` and will not be committed if you fork this repo
- **Never share your token with anyone** — it provides full access to your Discord account
